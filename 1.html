Please share your discomfort!

Feel free to share your usual discomfort and worries! Even the slightest thing is welcome! For example, "My ears hurt because of the mask these days." It's the same for everyone who has worries but can't tell an acquaintance. However, if you mention your discomfort on the website, there will be people who will feel better and worry about it together. Above all, this website places importance on interactive feedback.
